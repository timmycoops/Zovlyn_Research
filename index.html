Independent research for
lithium investors

The lithium space runs on press releases, unverified assumptions, and recycled broker decks. These tools exist so anyone willing to do the work can sanity-check the economics, compare projects on a consistent basis, and understand the mechanics of the exchanges these names trade on.

Every model is open — if you disagree with an assumption, change it. The methodology is calibrated against published feasibility studies and real M&A transactions. Where a company-specific deep dive exists, it's a worked example of the framework applied to a single name, not a recommendation.

All models and reports are independent research, pre-PEA estimates where applicable, and calibrated to comparable feasibility studies. Not investment advice. Figures sourced from company filings, NI 43-101 reports, and public M&A data. Do your own work.
