An independent mining research desk.

Zovlyn Research is a one-person operation building tools and writing notes about hard-rock mining — mostly lithium, with the system designed to expand to copper, uranium and other battery materials over time.

Everything here is built for an audience that already knows the vocabulary. NI 43-101, JORC, SK-1300, EV/t LCE, SC6 CIF — no glossary, no hand-holding. The goal is to do primary research more carefully than the sell-side does it, and to share the working out.

Tools are interactive. Reports are auditable. Numbers come with sources.
