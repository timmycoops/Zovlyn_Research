Cisco Lithium Project

Q2 Metals Corp (TSX.V: QTWO) released a maiden NI 43-101 MRE on April 20, 2026 for the Cisco Lithium Project in Eeyou Istchee James Bay, Quebec — one of the largest lithium pegmatite resources ever defined.

These tools were built to analyse the project's economics, understand where it sits relative to global peers, and model what different development scenarios might imply for shareholder value.

All models are pre-PEA estimates calibrated to comparable feasibility studies. Not investment advice. Figures sourced from company filings and public M&A data as of April 2026.
