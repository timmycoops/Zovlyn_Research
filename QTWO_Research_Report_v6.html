Project Overview

The Cisco Lithium Project is located in the Eeyou Istchee James Bay region of Quebec, Canada — the same geological corridor hosting Patriot Battery Metals' Shaakichiuwaanaan and Nemaska's Whabouchi. The project comprises 801 mineral claims totaling 41,253 hectares, with the main mineralized zone just 6.5 km from the Billy Diamond Highway.

Q2 Metals announced the inaugural NI 43-101 Inferred Mineral Resource Estimate on April 20, 2026, establishing Cisco as one of the largest undeveloped hard rock lithium deposits globally.

Geology

Single continuous spodumene pegmatite body. Strike length: 1.8 km. True thickness ranges from 2m to over 450m. Near-surface with potential for large-scale open pit. Hosted in Archaean metasediments of the La Grande geological sub-province.

Peer Valuation — EV / t LCE

At ~$7/t LCE on an enterprise value basis, QTWO trades at a significant discount to peers. PMET peaked at ~$50/t LCE pre-resource. Post-MRE, the re-rate thesis hinges on the market pricing Cisco's 9.1Mt contained LCE closer to peer multiples as the project de-risks through PEA and eventually FS.

Cost Curve Position — Where Would Cisco Sit?

Benchmarking against Quebec peers, Cisco would likely sit in the $650-800/t SC6 range on the hard rock cost curve — comfortably below the marginal producer but above the Tier 1 Australian operations. Scale advantage (295Mt, potentially 20+ year mine life) supports lower unit costs than smaller James Bay projects.

Key Catalysts
MRE Published (Apr 2026) ✓

295Mt @ 1.36% Li2O. 9.1Mt contained LCE. Pit + underground constrained. De-risks tonnage and grade.

PEA / Scoping Study

Expected to follow MRE. Will establish preliminary economics, mine plan, CAPEX, and annual production targets. Critical catalyst for re-rate.

Metallurgical Test Work

Spodumene recovery rates, concentrate quality (SC5.5 vs SC6), and process flowsheet. Required for offtake discussions.

2026 Drill Program

Resource expansion beyond MRE footprint. Infill to upgrade Inferred → Indicated. Additional exploration target of 44-67Mt.

Lithium Price

SC6 recovered from $610 trough to ~$2,300. Structural deficit emerging supports further recovery. JPM forecasts spodumene doubling by Q4 2026.

M&A / Strategic

James Bay attracting major interest (Rio Tinto acquired Arcadium, Albemarle expanding). Cisco's scale makes it a logical target post-PEA.
