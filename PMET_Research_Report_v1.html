[01] ASSET & OPERATOR Shaakichiuwaanaan · Eeyou Istchee · QC

The Shaakichiuwaanaan Project sits in the Eeyou Istchee James Bay region of Québec, immediately adjacent to the Trans-Taïga Road and Hydro-Québec's high-voltage corridor — the same geology that hosts Nemaska's Whabouchi and Q2 Metals' Cisco. The CV5 pegmatite is the lead asset and the subject of the 20 October 2025 lithium-only Feasibility Study; CV13 and other dykes along strike sit outside the FS as Mineral Resource only.

PMET Resources — the post-rebrand entity of Patriot Battery Metals — is dual-listed on the TSX (PMET) and ASX (PMT), with US ADRs trading as PMETF. The rebrand was announced 2025 alongside the broadening of the asset thesis to include the Rigel caesium discovery — now defined as the world's largest pollucite-hosted Cs2O resource — and the Helios caesium zone identified in March 2026.

[02] CV5 LITHIUM-ONLY FEASIBILITY STUDY Eff. 20-Oct-2025 · NI 43-101 filed 14-Nov-2025

The FS is the project's first feasibility-grade economic study, replacing the August 2024 PEA. It is lithium-only: caesium and tantalum revenue are explicitly excluded, both from production and from economic returns. The headline number — US$1.19B after-tax NPV(8%) — is anchored on a US$1,221/t SC5.5 price deck, which is sober relative to peer studies and sits near long-term consensus rather than spot.

[03] MINE PLAN, RESERVES & RESOURCES CV5 · NI 43-101 · Probable

CV5 is mined as a hybrid open-pit + underground operation. Six sub-pits feed the mill at an overall strip ratio of 3.4 : 1. A higher-grade underground component using long-hole open stoping lifts steady-state output to ~800 ktpa SC5.5. The FS production target is underpinned solely by Probable Reserves — no Inferred material is in the 19-year plan.

[04] ECONOMICS & SENSITIVITY Constant CAD · unlevered · post-tax

The FS is sensitive in the order you'd expect: price, then capex, then opex. A +20% bump in SC5.5 price lifts after-tax NPV(8%) by ~70% to ~US$2.0B; the AISC floor of US$597/t SC5.5 is genuinely defensible — well below where peer hard-rock projects sit, and significantly below where the marginal Australian operations currently produce. Initial gross capex of C$1.98B falls to C$1.51B (US$1.126B) once tax credits and pre-production revenue are deducted, materially improving the financing math.

[05] PEER POSITIONING · COST CURVE

[06.1] KEY RISKS
CAPITAL · QUANTUMC$1.98B gross / C$1.51B net is large relative to PMET's market cap. Financing risk is the single biggest near-term overhang — equity dilution, project debt covenants, or strategic dilution are all on the table.
PRICEFS sensitivity to SC5.5 price is high — +20% price → +70% NPV. Conversely, sustained lithium spot below the US$1,221/t base case meaningfully compresses returns and could push out FID.
PERMITTING TIMELINEESIA filed April 2026; permits typically take 12–24 months. Slippage delays H2 2027 FID. Cree Nation consent and federal review (Impact Assessment Agency of Canada) are both required.
EXECUTIONGreenfield project in a remote James Bay setting. Construction labour, supply chain, weather, and tailings management all in scope. 11% capex contingency is reasonable but not large for a project of this size.
FXRevenue is USD, costs predominantly CAD. CAD strength compresses margin; the FS does not disclose a price-FX matrix in the press release headline.
RESERVE BASIS100% Probable (no Proven). FS conversion from Indicated → Probable is the norm but means continued sterilisation and stope-design refinement during early production years.
SCOPEFS is lithium-only at CV5. Investors should not double-count Rigel / Helios caesium value or CV13 resource until separate studies or formal resource expansion is published.
COUNTERPARTY · OFFTAKEPowerCo binding term sheet is constructive but binding term sheets are not signed long-form agreements. Volkswagen Group strategic position is a positive — also a constraint on M&A optionality.
