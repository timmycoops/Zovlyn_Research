The Zovlyn system.

A working design system for an independent mining research desk. Tokens, components and templates that any tool on this site — existing or future — should inherit from. Built terminal-first, content-second, decoration last.

OWNERS · Zovlyn VERSION · 1.0 STATUS · Live SCOPE · Web · dark only
[01] OVERVIEW read first

The Zovlyn system is built around three convictions:

1. Density is respect. Our audience already knows what NI 43-101, JORC, EV/t LCE and SC6 mean. They don't need glossaries, they need data per square inch. The system favours small type, monospace numbers, and terminal vocabulary because it treats the reader as a peer.

2. The accent earns its place. One signature color — lithium green — carries every emphasis. Multi-color "data slop" is forbidden. Status colors (green/red/amber/cyan) appear only when they signal something semantic.

3. Templates over tools. Every recurring artifact has a template: home, research report, calculator, screener, map, primer. Once a template is defined, new content fills it rather than re-deriving the design.

When in doubt: copy index.html or QTWO_Research_Report_v7.html as a starting point. They are the canonical implementations.

[02] VOICE & TONE
DO

"At ~$7/t LCE on an EV basis, QTWO trades at a significant discount to peers. PMET peaked at ~$50/t LCE pre-resource."

DON'T

"QTWO is an exciting opportunity in the high-growth lithium space! With its world-class asset, the company is well-positioned to deliver shareholder value 🚀"

House voice is independent, contrarian, technical, calm. Sharp without being loud. State the number, state the comparison, state the caveat. No exclamation marks, no emoji, no superlatives that aren't earned by data.

Numbers are sacred. Always include unit, source, and as-of date when context allows. $2,000/t SC6, not "around $2k". 295 Mt @ 1.36% Li2O, not "huge resource".

"Not investment advice" is non-negotiable on every page that discusses a position or company.

[03] NAMING
In the product

Tools are named by what they do, not codenames. EV / t LCE Universe, not LCE-08. The brand is "ZOVLYN RESEARCH" written as a quiet badge in nav, never the page title.

In files

Production files use snake_case or kebab-case: lithium_supply_model.html, cisco-enhanced-v2.html. Versioned files (v6, v7) are kept until the new version is verified, then the old one is removed.

In type

Tickers always uppercase with exchange suffix: QTWO.V, PMET.TO. Units are SI with the standard chemistry suffixes: Li2O, Li2CO3, SC6.

[04] COLOR click any swatch to copy
Surfaces · the canvas

Five tones from deepest (page) to lightest (table-row hover). Stay in this lane. Never invent a sixth gray.

Lines · separation
Foreground · text on dark
Accent · lithium green

One color does the work of three. Used for: brand badge, primary buttons, section numbers, "winning" data, hover, focus, KPI emphasis, links, checkmarks.

Semantic · only when meaning is signaled

These are NOT decoration. --green = positive change/cash flow. --red = negative/risk/alert. --amber = watch/caution/exogenous. --cyan = peer/neutral comparison.
