Standard Conversion Factors
1 kt SC6 (6% Li₂O) = 0.1484 kt LCE (theoretical)
6.74 kt SC6 = 1 kt LCE (theoretical)
7.5 kt SC6 ≈ 1 kt LCE (industry, ~90% recovery)
1 kt LCE = 1.136 kt LiOH·H₂O
1 kt LCE = 0.188 kt Li metal
1% Li₂O in ore = 2.473% Li₂CO₃ equivalent
1 kt Li metal = 5.323 kt LCE
All from molecular weights: Li₂CO₃=73.89, Li₂O=29.88, LiOH·H₂O=41.96, Li=6.94
